LimitLock Support

Tournament fish culling, fast.

How It Works

Set your fish limit, log each catch with a weight and a color or number tag, and LimitLock keeps your bag sorted. When you boat a bigger fish with a full bag, it tells you exactly which one to cull.

Voice Logging

Tap the microphone and say the weight followed by the color — for example, "3.52 yellow." LimitLock will log the catch automatically. Make sure you've granted microphone and speech recognition permissions in Settings.

Apple Watch

The companion Watch app syncs automatically with your phone. You can log catches and view your bag from either device.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Voice isn't working. Go to Settings → LimitLock and make sure Microphone and Speech Recognition are both enabled.
  • Watch isn't syncing. Make sure both devices are nearby and the Watch app is open. Data syncs in real time when reachable, or queues up via user info transfer.
  • I entered the wrong weight. Tap any fish in the list to edit its weight or change its tag.
  • Can I change my limit mid-tournament? The limit locks once you log your first fish to prevent accidental changes. Reset to change it.
