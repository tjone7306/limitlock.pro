What it does

Built for tournament days

Every feature exists to do one thing: save seconds on the water, so you can keep your hands on the rod.

🎙️
Voice logging
Say the weight and color tag out loud — "3.52 yellow" — and LimitLock does the rest. No fumbling with your phone on a rocking boat.
⚖️
Auto-cull
Your bag stays sorted heaviest-to-lightest. When you boat a bigger fish, LimitLock tells you exactly which one to release.
🏆
Live tournaments
Run a tournament with your buddies. Share an invite code, and every angler's bag and Big-5 ranks live on one leaderboard.
⌚
Apple Watch
Log catches, check your total, and see your sorted bag from your wrist. Stays in sync with your phone automatically.
📊
Day stats
Tracks every fish you boat — not just the ones in your bag. End the day knowing exactly how your bite was.
Fish with friends

Run a live tournament with your buddies.

Open a tournament, share the invite code, and every angler logs into the same live leaderboard. Each angler sees their own bag — the leaderboard ranks everyone by Big-5 weight, updating the moment someone boats a fish.

  • 1
    Create or join in seconds. One six-character invite code is all your buddies need.
  • 2
    Big-5 leaderboard. Everyone's top five, ranked by total weight, live on the boat.
  • 3
    Activity feed. See when your buddy boats a giant — and exactly how much weight you need.
  • 4
    Photo requests. Tap any catch to ask the angler for a quick verification pic.
